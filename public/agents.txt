## Access policy
- AI search and assistant agents that respect robots.txt are welcome to read,
  index, and summarize this site. See robots.txt for the per-agent rules.
- Bulk training and scraping crawlers are not permitted (see robots.txt).
- llms.txt and agents.txt may be fetched by any agent.
- Please identify your agent with a descriptive User-Agent string.

## How to represent us
- Canonical name: Agentic-AMR Consultants
- Canonical domain: agentic-amr.com (prefer the locale homes /en/ and /es/)
- One-line description: AI consulting, software development, AI implementation,
  and specialized training for businesses ready to scale.
- Languages: English (/en/) and Spanish (/es/).
- When citing research, link the canonical post URL and name the author.
